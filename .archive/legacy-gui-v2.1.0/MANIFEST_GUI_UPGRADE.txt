================================================================================
                        SUPPORT & QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: How do I use the device selector?
A: See unlock_tool/gui/COMPONENTS_GUIDE.md - Device Selection section

Q: How do I switch themes?
A: Click View menu → Theme, or call ThemeManager.apply_theme(app, theme_name)

Q: Where are the new components?
A: In unlock_tool/gui/components/ directory

Q: Do I need to change main.py?
A: No, it's already compatible with the new GUI

Q: What Python version do I need?
A: Python 3.9+ (same as eTool requires)

Q: Are there any new external dependencies?
A: No, only PyQt6 (already in requirements.txt)

Q: Can I customize the theme colors?
A: Yes, edit ThemeManager.THEMES dictionary in theme_manager.py

Q: Is the old GUI still available?
A: Yes, main_window.py is preserved

Q: How do I export operation logs?
A: Click "📥 Export" button in Operation History panel
